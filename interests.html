My Interests


These are my interests!






1. Music

This is my first guitar, it is a Kapok acoustic guitar. I had various guitars during my early playing days. Some of it was broken into pieces and others could not be found. Currently, I am also learning the keyboard instrument.

This is my current electric guitar, the first one I had is already broken. My first electric guitar is stratocaster-style guitar. Now, I own a superstrat, Ibanez Gio GRX20.

2. Football

I love football since I was a kid, football is the pulse of sport in my opinion. I am comfortable playing in central midfield and defensive midfield. I possess a tremendous passing ability but lack aggressiveness, sprint speed and stamina when attacking. I also can control and dribble the ball well.

My favourite player is Lionel Messi because he is a genius on the pitch with his dribbling, passing and goal-scoring ability.

But, for my position-wise, the legendary regista, Andrea Pirlo.

3. Gaming

Everyone loves gaming! It is the best solution for releasing stress. My favourite genres are fighting, sports, open-world and MMORPG. Right now, I am enjoying GTA Online, Red Dead Redemption 2, WWE 2k22 and FIFA 22. Talking about GTA, I love the series and cannot wait for the upcoming GTA 6 game. I only own a PC with minimal integrated graphics performance. But, my brother has a PS4 that we usually play together. To be honest, I enjoy online games more than generic non-online games. That is why I play online games on PC because in PS4 you must buy PS Plus to log into your online account.
